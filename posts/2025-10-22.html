For some reason, I could not fall asleep last night until around 1:00am. The result was I woke up late this morning which resulted in coming late into the office around 11:00am. BUT.....I did come in which is an improvement. Only 1 more day and that is three days this week. I need to improve my bedtime routine somehow so I can break this bad habit.

I have been doing research recently on fast dopamine versus slow dopamine. Fast dopamine examples are caffeine, video games, etc. while slow dopamine examples are things like DIY projects, reading, self-improvement, etc. I am VERY guilty of falling victim to mindless scrolling, watching TV, or playing video games when there are a million other things I should be doing. I need to figure out a way to "remap" or rewire my brain to replace these types of activities.

Or more 3D printing? I don't know but I do not feel excited anymore about much of anything. Just stuck and stagnant. I will not give up though. I am not built to give up.
