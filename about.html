How to add new entries

  1. Duplicate a file in /posts and rename it with YYYY-MM-DD-title.html.
  2. Edit the <article> content.
  3. Add a new list item to index.html pointing to your new post.

Tip: Because you use Obsidian, consider drafting entries in Markdown, then paste into this template and keep your content portable.
